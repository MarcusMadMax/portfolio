About

This was a love job for a former work colleague who started his own property maintainance business. When I approached him he was quite unsure on how a website would help his business grow, as he expanded his customer base by word to mouth. After further consultation and presentations of some successful examples on how an online presence could benefit his company he was convinced and got aboard.

The Idea

The idea was to create a simple and down to earth webpage to refelect the customers brand colors and logos and was easy to navigate.

Technical specificatios

Again on the first page there is a slider created with Bootstrap. The navigation is in the top center whereas the "Get free quote" button will lead to the contact page. Animations on scrolling are created by implementing the "aos" library. The convertion to a Wordpress theme, to give the customer the option to change text and images where needed, was pretty straight forward here. Unfortunatly the site is not online at the moment due to family growth on the customers side but will be in due time.

Navigation on mobile

The navigation on mobile devices is pretty straight forward with the "Hamburger" converting to a cross and the menu slides down when clicked.
