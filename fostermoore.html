What is Verne®?

Verne (formerly known as Catalyst Next Generation or Catalyst NG) takes the fundamentals and knowledge invested in nine years of Catalyst, plus the advances in technology over that time, and distils them into a simpler and more modular architecture. The advent of modern software packages such as document databases, reliable messaging (Rabbit) and Spring boot enables us to build a single application that is cloud ready, multi tenanted, an always online register for our clients. Correspondingly, the review and revamp of original design and configuration concepts enables us to provide an easier to use, more intuitive and transparent development/configuration environment that will result in the quicker development of projects, that are more robust, less prone to trouble with upgrades and easier to support.

Projects

Communications

"Communications" are the messages sent via a range of channels from the product, including within the product as alerts. Business service and compliance workflows trigger them, and they are compiled using templates and related settings. The delivery log provides a record of them when they are sent and supports re-sending if necessary.

Fee management

"Fee Management" is an autonomous service within Verne that is leveraged by any register implementation that requires the generation of charges for its business services.

Data model

The Verne® "data model" is a feature that allows to define and maintain a data dictionary for the registry data, as well as to enforce data standards in the physical database layer. Verne data model is a repository of information about registry data such as meaning, relationships to other data, format and usage. It is a central point in ensuring the overall accuracy, completeness, transparency and consistency of the registry data.
