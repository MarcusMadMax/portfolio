About

KCC (Kids Conversation Club) was my submission for the "UX" summative. The wire framing and design was done in Adobe XD.

The Idea

The idea was to create a modern application not only in the looking but also in functionality.

Technical specifications

I have used advanced HTML, SCSS as well as introductory JavaScript to create interactive interfaces. With SCSS I made use of variables to simplify the assignment of colors and fonts. As the design of the APP is fairly mixed yet plain, as the target audience is a younger audience, I used mixins very sporadic as most pages have different backgrounds and colors. The font used is consistently over the pages.

Wire frames & Navigation

Although the linkage of all the different pages seems quite complex the functionality of the app was quite simple really. From the landing page all the links where clear and bold and would help navigate throughout the entire app. Images acted as an introduction for the content and guide the user through the app.
