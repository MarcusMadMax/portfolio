About

This was a love job for a friend of mine. He is a professional photographer with a great passion for the trade. The wire framing and design was done in Figma. I used HTML plus css for the frame work to build components and JavaScript to enhance user experience. After that I created a custom Wordpress theme I called "Portfolio". The theme offers the possibility to update relevant information by the client.

The Idea

The idea was to create a modern webpage not only in the looking but also in functionality.

Technical specificatios

First thing you will see when you navigate to this site is a slider created with Bootstrap. On mobile this will take up the whole screen so I added an arrow to move the slider out of view, I thought I'll add a little challenge for the user. The same applies for when you navigate to the full screen view of the photographs. Easier on desktop as the menu stays always in sight on the left hand site. The various sections slide in from different angels. In full screen mode on desktop you can navigate trough the images by clicking left or righ and clicking in the middle will take you back again. You can check it out here.

Navigation on mobile

The navigation on mobile devices was a bit of a challenge as we have full screen images and they shouldn't be spoiled by a navigation overlaying them. So I came up with the idea of the rotating arrow on the top left. This can cause challenges for users and I will have to tackle accesibility issues in future iterations.
